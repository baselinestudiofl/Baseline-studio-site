Pricing

Straight answers
on what it costs.

Most production companies make you fill out a form just to see a number. We don't do that. Here's a real sense of what things cost — so you can plan before we even talk. Every event gets a custom proposal, but these ranges give you somewhere to start.

How It Works

No surprise
invoices.

Every proposal we send is a line-item breakdown — what's included, what each piece costs, and what the total is. We don't add fees after you sign. If you want to add something, we price it separately and you decide. No surprises at the final invoice.

Add-Ons

Build your own package.

Cold Spark Fountains

Two units, indoor-safe, with setup and timed detonation. Perfect for first dances and grand entrances.

CO₂ Cannons

High-volume CO2 blast on cue. Electrifying for dance floors and big-reveal moments.

Dancing on Cloud

Low-lying water-based fog for first dances and entrances. Stays below knee height.

Bistro String Lights

Warm Edison-style overhead strands. Priced per 50-foot run, outdoor-rated.

LED Video Wall

P3.9 indoor panels. Custom content playback, scenic backdrops, IMAG feeds.

Gobo Projection

Your name, logo, or monogram projected onto walls, floors, or ceilings.

Live Musicians

Saxophone, violin, guitar, piano — solo or ensemble. Paired with our sound system.

Confetti Cannons

Custom-color confetti bursts for countdowns, reveals, and big moments.

These are starting points. Final pricing depends on your event length, venue, equipment scope, travel, and crew size. We always send a full custom proposal before anything is signed — you'll see the exact breakdown, not a ballpark. If something doesn't make sense in the quote, just ask us.
