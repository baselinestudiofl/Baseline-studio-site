Pricing

Built around
your event.

A wedding isn't a corporate gala isn't a quinceañera. Every event has a different flow, a different feel, and different priorities. So instead of a fixed price list, we build a custom proposal around what your event actually needs. Here's what typically goes into each type.

How It Works

One quote.
Full breakdown.

Every proposal we send is a line-item breakdown — what's included, what each piece costs, and the total. Pricing depends on your event length, venue, and scope, so every quote is built from scratch. This page gives you a real sense of what goes into each type of event before we talk.

Add-Ons

Mix and match what you need.

Cold Spark Fountains

Two units, indoor-safe, with setup and timed detonation. Perfect for first dances and grand entrances.

CO₂ Cannons

High-volume CO2 blast on cue. Electrifying for dance floors and big-reveal moments.

Dancing on Cloud

Low-lying water-based fog for first dances and entrances. Stays below knee height.

Bistro String Lights

Warm Edison-style overhead strands. Priced per 50-foot run, outdoor-rated.

LED Video Wall

P3.9 indoor panels. Custom content playback, scenic backdrops, IMAG feeds.

Gobo Projection

Your name, logo, or monogram projected onto walls, floors, or ceilings.

Live Musicians

Saxophone, violin, guitar, piano — solo or ensemble. Paired with our sound system.

Confetti Cannons

Custom-color confetti bursts for countdowns, reveals, and big moments.

Every quote is custom and built around your specific event. Final pricing depends on event length, venue, equipment scope, crew size, and any travel. Event details sometimes change — a longer timeline, a different venue layout, last-minute additions — and that can affect the final cost. We flag anything that does before it's on your invoice. You'll always see the full breakdown before you sign anything.
