Frequently Asked Questions

How much do bistro string lights cost for an event in Miami?

Bistro string light rentals in Miami typically range from $600 to $2,500+ depending on coverage area, installation complexity, and whether the space is indoor or outdoor. Baseline Studio provides custom quotes based on your venue's dimensions and setup requirements.

What's the difference between bistro lights and fairy lights?

Bistro lights (also called Edison lights or café lights) use large, warm globe bulbs on heavy-duty cable — they create a rich, warm glow visible from across a room. Fairy lights are thin wire with tiny LED bulbs, better suited for decorative accents. For event canopies, bistro lights are the industry standard.

Can bistro string lights be used outdoors in Miami?

Yes. Our outdoor-rated bistro lights are weatherproof and safe for open-air events, outdoor receptions, garden weddings, and waterfront venues. We rig them using professional-grade poles, posts, or existing structures — no permanent attachments required.

How much area can bistro lights cover?

A typical bistro light canopy covers 20x20 feet to 40x60 feet depending on strand length and rigging pattern. We'll measure your space and map out the configuration in advance so you know exactly what the finished look will be before the event day.

Do you handle setup and teardown of the string lights?

Yes, completely. Our crew handles rigging, power connection, and full breakdown after your event. We coordinate with your venue and other vendors so installation is completed before guests arrive. You don't touch a single wire — just enjoy the atmosphere.
