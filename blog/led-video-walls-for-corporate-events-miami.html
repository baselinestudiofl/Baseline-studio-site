Corporate Events

The Complete Guide to LED Video Walls for Corporate Events in Miami

By Saul Leon, Founder Published January 20, 2026 Baseline Studio — Miami, FL

An LED video wall transforms a corporate event from a presentation into an immersive experience. Live feeds of your keynote speaker. Branded animations. Real-time attendee content. Data visualizations. LED video walls have become essential for modern corporate events, product launches, and galas in all of South Florida.

What Are LED Video Walls?

LED video walls are modular panels of light-emitting diodes arranged together to create a large, bright display. Unlike projection, which requires darkness and has limited brightness, LED walls are vibrant in any lighting condition. They can be configured into any size or shape — a 10x6-foot wall, a 20x12-foot statement display, a curved wall, even circular or irregular shapes.

Key Technical Terms Explained

  • Pixel Pitch: The distance between pixels. Lower numbers mean higher resolution. 3mm pitch is ultra-sharp; 6mm pitch is still crisp for viewing from 10+ feet away. Most corporate events use 4–6mm pitch.
  • Brightness (nits): How bright the display is. Corporate events typically need 2,000–4,000 nits depending on ambient lighting.
  • Refresh Rate: Professional LED walls refresh at 1,920–3,840 Hz, creating flicker-free video.
  • Viewing Distance: Smaller pixel pitch means people can sit closer to the wall.

LED Walls vs. Projection

Projection is cheaper upfront but has major limitations. Projectors need darkened rooms — not possible during afternoon corporate events. Projectors create a flat, 2D image that blends with the background. LED walls are bright, vibrant, and three-dimensional, drawing attention in any lighting condition. For corporate events where first impressions matter, LED wins every time.

IMAG: Image Magnification for Live Events

IMAG is a critical use case for corporate events. Cameras capture your keynote speaker, and the LED wall shows their face 20 feet tall. Audiences in the back see every expression. It creates intimacy and engagement. IMAG requires professional cameras positioned strategically, a video switcher to choose which camera feeds to the wall, a live video engineer, and audio synchronized with video.

Corporate Branding Opportunities

An LED wall is a branding canvas. Your company logo animated during entry. Brand colors in the background. Product photography as attendees arrive. Live social media feeds. Real-time sponsor recognition. Animated transitions between speakers. These elements make events feel premium and intentional.

Sizing Guide for Different Room Sizes

  • Small (under 200 guests, under 4,000 sq ft): 10x6-foot wall. Adequate for 200–300 viewers.
  • Medium (200–500 guests, 4,000–8,000 sq ft): 16x9-foot wall. Visible from all seating areas.
  • Large (500+ guests, 8,000+ sq ft): 20x12-foot or larger. Create visual impact in cavernous ballrooms.
  • Very Large (1,000+ guests, outdoor): 24x14-foot or multiple walls for outdoor events.

Cost vs. Impact

LED video walls are a significant production investment — but one that pays for itself in how the event feels. It keeps attendees engaged during speeches, amplifies your branding, and creates content for social media. Events with LED walls feel bigger, more professional, and more memorable. Frequently Asked Questions

How much does an LED video wall cost for a Miami corporate event?

LED video wall rental pricing depends on wall size, pixel pitch, and event duration. Larger walls with tighter pixel pitch cost more but look dramatically better in lit environments. We quote every corporate event based on your specific space — reach out for a proposal.

What pixel pitch LED wall should I use for my event?

For indoor events where guests are 10–20 feet from the screen, a 2.6mm–3.9mm pixel pitch provides excellent clarity. For stages where guests are 20–50 feet back, a 3.9mm–5mm pitch is sufficient and more cost-effective. Outdoor events use larger pixel pitches due to ambient light.

What is IMAG at a corporate event?

IMAG (Image MAGnification) is the live video feed of speakers or performers displayed on the LED wall so everyone in the room — even in the back — can see clearly. It requires a camera operator and a video switcher in addition to the LED wall itself.
