Frequently Asked Questions

How much do live musicians cost for a wedding in Miami?

Live musicians for Miami events typically range from $800 for a solo performer to $3,500+ for a full ensemble depending on the instrument, number of musicians, set length, and travel requirements. Baseline Studio provides custom quotes — contact us with your event date and vision.

What instruments do you offer for live music at events?

We offer saxophone, violin, guitar, piano, trumpet, percussion, and string ensembles. A solo saxophonist is popular for cocktail hours; violin duos for ceremonies; full ensembles for dinner receptions. We'll recommend the right instrumentation for your event's flow and guest count.

Can live musicians perform alongside the DJ?

Yes — a live musician performing over DJ-mixed tracks is one of the most popular setups for Miami weddings and galas. The DJ handles the full set while a saxophonist or violinist improvises live on top. The result is a more dynamic, high-end experience than either alone.

Can you play specific songs or genres?

Yes. Our musicians perform across genres — jazz, classical, pop, Latin, R&B, and contemporary hits. For ceremonies, we can perform your processional and recessional songs. For receptions and cocktail hours, we build a set list around your preferences and vibe.

How far in advance should I book live musicians in Miami?

We recommend booking live musicians 4–8 months ahead for peak season weddings (October–May) in Miami. Specific musicians and ensembles book quickly for weekend dates. Corporate events and off-peak dates can often be accommodated with 6–8 weeks notice.
