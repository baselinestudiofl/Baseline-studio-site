Large events need visual information in more than one place. Overflow rooms, registration areas, cocktail spaces, and secondary stages all benefit from display screens that carry your event's content beyond the main stage.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does TV screen rental cost in Miami?

TV and display screen rental in Miami typically ranges from $200 for a single 55-inch screen to $1,500+ for multi-screen configurations with stands and content management. Baseline Studio provides quotes based on screen count, size, placement, and whether content loop setup is needed.

What screen sizes do you offer for event rentals?

We offer screens from 55 inches to 98 inches for table-level and eye-level display. For larger applications, we recommend our LED video wall panels. TV displays are ideal for venue directories, sponsor loops, cocktail hour slideshows, and breakout room presentations.

Can you manage the content that plays on the screens?

Yes. We can load and loop your content — slideshows, video reels, sponsor logos, social feeds, or event programs — on all screens. Content should be provided in advance in standard formats (MP4, JPEG, PNG, or Google Slides). We handle all the technical playback management on-site.

Can TV screens be used outdoors?

Standard commercial displays are not weatherproof and require shade and protection from direct sun. For outdoor events, we recommend our LED panels, which are weatherproof and readable in full daylight. Contact us and we'll recommend the right display technology for your outdoor setup.

What types of events use TV screen rentals?

TV screen rentals are popular at corporate conferences, trade shows, award ceremonies, product launches, and wedding receptions with photo slideshows. Multiple screens placed strategically around a venue ensure every guest has a clear view of your content regardless of where they're seated.
