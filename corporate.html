What We Do

DJ, AV, lighting,
and effects — one crew.

There's a hierarchy to corporate event production. The AV has to work. That's the baseline. After that, lighting is what transforms a venue from how it looks on a regular Tuesday into something that actually feels like an event. Then comes the DJ, the LED wall, the effects, the live music. We've done enough galas, product launches, and award dinners in South Florida to know what order that matters in and how to get all of it right on the same night.

Live Sound & AV

Line arrays, subs, handhelds and lavs for presenters, panelists, and performers. We spec the system based on your room and headcount, not just drop in a PA and hope for the best. From a 50-person boardroom to a 1,000-guest gala, clarity is non-negotiable. We test everything before anyone walks in.

LED Video Walls

P3.9 indoor LED panels in whatever size and configuration your stage needs. Brand content, presentation slides, event graphics, live camera feeds: all sources managed and switched live. We've done installs where the wall is the focal point of the entire room. We know how to make it look right.

Branded Lighting

Uplighting in your exact brand colors. Your logo projected on walls and floors. Stage lighting for speakers and award presentations. Lighting is honestly the highest-impact item per dollar in corporate event production. It's what makes a room go from ordinary to something worth photographing.

Common Questions

Corporate event production — FAQ.

How much does corporate event production in Miami cost?

It ranges from around $2,500 for a DJ-only setup to $15,000 or more for full production: LED video walls, branded lighting, live musicians, effects, and AV. The number depends on event size, venue, duration, and what you're going for. We build a custom quote for every event. No pre-set packages, because every event is different.

Do you provide both a DJ and live musicians for corporate events?

Yes, and it all runs through our own system. Jazz trios, Latin bands, string quartets, vocalists: whatever fits your event. The live set runs through our sound system, so there's no handoff gap when you transition from entertainment to the DJ set or the awards program. One crew handles the whole thing.

What's typically included in a corporate event package?

Every package starts with a professional DJ, full sound system, and microphones for speakers and presenters. From there it depends on the event: LED video walls, uplighting, branded lighting, logo projection, cold spark fountains, CO2 jets, and live musicians are all add-ons based on your needs and budget. We handle venue coordination, setup, and load-out.

How far in advance should we book?

For most corporate events, 4 to 8 weeks is ideal. For larger galas or high-demand venues, 2 to 4 months gives us room to lock in crew and equipment. That said, reach out even if your event is coming up soon. Depending on the date and scope, we can usually make it work.

Do you handle AV and presentation support for conferences?

Yes. We provide full AV support for corporate conferences in Miami: line array sound systems, wireless mics for presenters and panelists, and LED video wall or projector setups for presentation content. We can run IMAG feeds for larger venues and we coordinate directly with your event team on the run of show. Everything is tested before the first guest walks in.
