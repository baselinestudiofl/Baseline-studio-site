About This Venue

Miami Airport Convention Center

The Miami Airport Convention Center sits at 2701 NW 42nd Ave, a few minutes from Miami International Airport. That proximity is the whole point for most clients using it. When your attendees are flying in from across the country or internationally, you want a venue they can get to without a 45-minute Uber. The MACC handles that.

The facility runs large. Convention floors, ballrooms, breakout rooms, and pre-function spaces that can be combined or divided depending on what your event needs. Corporate conferences, trade shows, awards galas, product launches. That's the core of what this venue does and what we specialize in here.

Convention center production is a different discipline than hotel ballroom work. The rooms are bigger, the AV requirements are more complex, and the coordination with in-house teams takes more lead time. We know how to manage that. Every event here starts with a detailed site plan before anything gets loaded in.

What Makes This Venue Work

Scale and access. If your guests are traveling and you need a facility that can handle several hundred people or more, the MACC is a serious option. The rooms have the infrastructure for real production, not a workaround setup. We size our rig to the space and it works the way it's supposed to.

Load-In and Coordination

Convention centers have structured load-in procedures and we work within them. We're in contact with the venue's operations team well before your event date to confirm timing, power pulls, house AV integration, and anything else that needs to be nailed down in advance. You won't be managing those conversations.

FAQ

Questions About Events at Miami Airport Convention Center

Does the Miami Airport Convention Center have in-house AV? Do you work with it?
Yes. The MACC has in-house AV. We coordinate with their team before the event to confirm what integrates and what we bring ourselves. That conversation happens early so nothing is figured out the day of.
Do you do corporate conferences and galas at the MACC?
Yes. This is what the venue is designed for and what we do most here. LED video walls, IMAG camera systems, professional audio for keynotes, branded lighting. If you have a specific production need for your conference or gala, tell us and we'll tell you if we can do it.
Can you handle large-scale events at the MACC?
Yes. The MACC has the space for large-format events and we have the gear to fill it. Multi-room setups, high-capacity ballrooms, convention floors. We build a production plan specific to your layout and scale it from there.
Does the MACC allow special effects like cold sparks?
Special effects need prior approval and fire safety sign-off at the MACC. We handle the submission process as part of our event prep. Cold sparks are typically approvable. Fog and CO2 depend on the specific space. We confirm what's cleared before your event date.
Do you handle product launches and conferences at the MACC?
Yes. Product launches and conferences are a regular part of our work at convention venues. LED walls for presentations, IMAG for speaker feeds, wireless mics, professional audio for large rooms. We handle the technical build-out and run it the day of.
Why do corporate clients use the Miami Airport Convention Center?
Location mostly. It's close to the airport, which matters when your attendees are flying in. The venue has the room capacity and flexibility for events that outgrow hotel ballrooms. For companies running national conferences, trade shows, or large corporate galas, it's a practical choice that handles the logistics well.
