DJ Services Miami —
the DJ who reads the room.

The DJ is the night. When it's right, people don't leave the floor. When it's off, the whole room feels it. We read how the energy is moving — cocktail hour sounds different than dinner, dinner sounds different than the dance floor. We know when to push and when to let it breathe. MC available for ceremonies and grand entrances.

Get a Quote View All Services

We run Pioneer CDJ-3000s and the DJM-A9 mixer — the same rig you'd find at a serious club. Not because we're flexing, but because cheap gear skips, lags, and fails at the worst possible times. Your guests won't notice the equipment. They'll just notice that the music never stopped.

Open format is our specialty. Tell us the vibe — Latin, hip-hop, top 40, EDM, throwbacks — we'll blend it into something that actually feels intentional. We also do genre-specific sets if your crowd skews one way hard. Either way, we talk to you before the event, not just take a form and show up.

MC service is available and makes a bigger difference than most people expect. A good MC moves a reception from event to experience. We've worked with couples who wanted full MC energy and coordinators who just needed someone to make clean announcements — we adjust. What we don't do is talk too much. The music is the point.

And if the run of show changes three times between dinner and the first dance — which happens at almost every wedding — we adapt. That's the job.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do DJ services cost in Miami?

DJ services in Miami typically range from $1,200 to $3,500+ depending on event length, equipment needs, and date. Baseline Studio provides custom quotes based on your specific event — contact us for exact pricing for your wedding, corporate event, or party.

How far in advance should I book a DJ in Miami?

For weddings and large events in Miami, we recommend booking 6–12 months in advance, especially for peak season (October–May). Corporate events and parties can often be accommodated with 4–8 weeks notice. Popular Saturday dates fill quickly — reach out early.

Do you bring your own DJ equipment?

Yes. Baseline Studio arrives with a complete professional setup: Pioneer CDJ-3000 media players, DJM mixer, powered speakers, subwoofers, and all cabling. You don't need to source any audio equipment — we handle setup and breakdown entirely.

What types of events does Baseline Studio DJ?

We DJ weddings, quinceañeras, corporate galas, birthday parties, brand activations, and private events across Miami, Coral Gables, Brickell, Miami Beach, and South Florida. We adapt our style and approach to match the energy and formality of each event.

Can you take song requests and work with our playlist?

Absolutely. We work from a detailed questionnaire before every event so we know your must-play songs, do-not-play list, and overall vibe. We also read the room in real time — adjusting the energy to keep guests engaged from cocktail hour through the last dance.
