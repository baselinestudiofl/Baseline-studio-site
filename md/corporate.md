## Corporate Services

### Audio & Sound
- Full FOH (front-of-house) audio engineering
- Line array systems with subwoofers — QSC and JBL
- Wireless handheld and lapel microphone packages
- Podium microphones and confidence monitor setups
- Speaker support for keynotes, panels, and presentations
- Backup amplifiers and microphone systems at every event

### Lighting
- **LED Uplighting** — Venue walls washed in brand colors. Creates a polished, branded environment from the moment guests arrive.
- **Stage Lighting** — Moving heads, spotlights, and wash fixtures for speakers, award presentations, and live entertainment.
- **Gobo Projection** — Company logo or event name projected onto walls or floors.
- **Dancefloor Lighting** — For company parties and after-event receptions.

### Visual & Display
- **LED Video Walls** — P3.9 indoor panels for speaker support, branded content, IMAG feeds, and scenic backdrops.
- **TV & Display Screens** — Networked signage for agendas, sponsor recognition, and directional displays.
- **Event Projection** — Large-format screen projection for presentations and video playback.
- **Live Switching** — Multi-camera feed management for speaker presentations and live event feeds.

### Entertainment
- **Corporate DJ** — Background sets during cocktail and dining hours, or full open-format sets for company party receptions.
- **Live Musicians** — Saxophone, violin, guitar, or piano for cocktail hours and receptions. Elevated atmosphere without the volume of a band.

### Special Effects
- **CO2 Jets and Cannons** — High-energy dancefloor effects for company parties and after-parties.
- **Confetti Cannons** — Custom-color bursts for product reveals, countdowns, and celebrations.
- **Cold Spark Fountains** — Award ceremony moments, executive entrances, or brand reveals.
