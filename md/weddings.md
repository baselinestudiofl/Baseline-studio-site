## Special Effects for Weddings

**Cold Spark Fountains** — Gold sparkler effect for first dance or grand entrance. Indoor-safe. Two units, operated by our crew.

**Dancing on Cloud** — Low-lying fog for the first dance. The couple appears to float. Stays below knee height, dissipates cleanly.

**CO2 Jets** — High-energy CO2 burst for late-night dancefloor moments. Optional add-on.

**Gobo Projection** — The couple's names or monogram projected onto a wall, floor, or ceiling. Included in premium packages, add-on in others.
