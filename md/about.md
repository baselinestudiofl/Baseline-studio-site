Baseline Studio is a Miami-based event production company founded by Saul. We started as a DJ company and built from there — adding live sound, lighting, LED video walls, and special effects because clients kept needing them, and needing them done right under one roof.

---

## What We Are

A full-service event production company. That means we handle the audio, the lighting, the visual production, and the special effects — not just one piece of the event. One team, one proposal, one point of contact from planning through breakdown.

We work with couples planning weddings, corporate event managers, hotel venues, nonprofits, and families celebrating major events. The events change. The approach doesn't.

---

## How We Work

**We show up prepared.** Every event gets a custom run-of-show review before the day. We know the venue load-in, the coordinator's contact, the timeline, and every cued moment before we arrive.

**We carry backup.** Backup amplifiers, backup DJ controllers, backup microphone receivers — all on the truck. Equipment issues have never stopped one of our events. That's not luck. That's how we operate.

**We adapt.** Run-of-show changes happen at every event. A limo runs late, a toast gets added, a song gets swapped last minute. We handle it without involving the client. That's the whole point of experience.

**We don't surprise you on the invoice.** Every proposal is a line-item breakdown. What's in it is what you pay. If something changes, we price it separately and you approve it before it goes on the invoice.
