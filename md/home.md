## What We Do

Sound, lighting, and visual production — handled together by one team. Most companies do one or two of these well. We do all of it.

### DJ & Entertainment
Professional open-format DJ using Pioneer CDJ-3000s and a DJM-A9 mixer. MC service available. Music consultation before every event. We read the room and adjust — ceremony to cocktail hour to reception without missing a beat.

### Live Sound & Audio
Touring-grade FOH systems — QSC and JBL line arrays with subwoofers. Wireless microphone packages. Outdoor-rated for South Florida conditions. Every system is built for the space, not just plugged in and hoped for.

### Event Lighting
LED uplighting that transforms a venue's walls and pillars. Moving head fixtures that bring the dancefloor to life. Stage lighting for performance. Bistro string lights overhead. Lighting is the most underrated element of event design — the right setup changes everything about how a room feels.

### Special Effects
Cold spark fountains for first dances and grand entrances. CO2 jets and cannons for dancefloor energy. Dancing on cloud low-lying fog for ceremony and entrance moments. All indoor-safe, all handled by our crew.

### LED Video Walls & Projection
P3.9 indoor LED panels for scenic backdrops, IMAG feeds, custom content, and live switching. Gobo projection for names, monograms, and company logos onto walls, floors, or ceilings.
