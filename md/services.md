**URL:** https://www.baselinestudiofl.com/service-uplighting

## Special Effects

### Cold Spark Fountains
**URL:** https://www.baselinestudiofl.com/service-cold-spark

Indoor-safe cold spark sparkler effect — the gold fountain effect seen at first dances and grand entrances. Uses a proprietary titanium powder compound, not traditional fireworks. No fire, no smoke, no residue. Safe for indoor use at most South Florida venues. Standard package: two units, positioned and timed by our crew.

Most common uses: first dance sparkler moment, grand entrance, wedding toast moment, birthday reveal.

### CO2 Jets
**URL:** https://www.baselinestudiofl.com/service-co2-jets

Vertical CO2 burst effects, 10–15 feet high. Triggered on cue by our crew. Creates instant dancefloor energy. Most common uses: drop moments during a DJ set, countdown moments, event openers.

### CO2 Cannons
**URL:** https://www.baselinestudiofl.com/service-co2-cannon

High-volume horizontal CO2 blast — larger volume than jets, wide horizontal spread. Best for large dancefloors and high-energy moments. Operated by our crew.

### Dancing on Cloud
**URL:** https://www.baselinestudiofl.com/service-dancing-on-cloud

Low-lying water-based fog effect that stays below knee height on the dancefloor. Creates a cloud-walking visual for first dances and grand entrances. Not a traditional fog machine — the effect is visible at floor level and dissipates cleanly. No smoke smell, no ceiling fogging, no fire alarm risk when used correctly.

### Special Effects Package
**URL:** https://www.baselinestudiofl.com/service-fx

Combination of special effects elements customized per event. Typically includes 2–3 effect types timed to key moments: entrance, first dance, and peak dancefloor moments.

### LED Video Walls
**URL:** https://www.baselinestudiofl.com/service-led

P3.9 indoor LED panel walls. P3.9 pixel pitch means high resolution at close viewing distances — appropriate for wedding receptions, corporate events, and galas where guests are within 10–15 feet of the screen.

Use cases:
- Scenic backdrop behind the DJ or stage
- Custom event branding and animated content
- Photo/video slideshow display
- IMAG (image magnification) feed for large audiences
- Live camera switching for keynote presentations
- Corporate branded content and logo display

Content can be pre-loaded or live-switched during the event.
