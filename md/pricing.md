## How Pricing Works

1. You contact us with your event details — date, venue, approximate guest count, and what you're looking for.
2. We send a custom line-item proposal within 24 hours.
3. The proposal shows exactly what's included and what each element costs.
4. Nothing is added after you sign. If you want to add something later, it's priced separately and your approval is required before it goes on the invoice.
5. A deposit holds your date. Balance is due before the event.

There are no surprise fees. No "travel fees" buried in the fine print. No equipment charges that weren't in the original proposal.

---

## What Affects the Price

- **Event duration** — Hours of coverage from setup to breakdown
- **Venue** — Distance from Miami, load-in requirements, and power availability
- **Equipment scope** — Number of uplighting fixtures, size of LED wall, number of effect units
- **Crew size** — Full production events require multiple technicians on-site
- **Day of week and season** — Peak season in South Florida is October through April
