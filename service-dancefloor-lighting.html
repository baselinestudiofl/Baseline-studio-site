Dance Floor Lighting Miami —
a dance floor worth dancing on.

Moving heads, LED beams, wash lights above the dance floor. The kind of lighting that makes people feel like they're actually somewhere — not just a party in a banquet hall. That difference usually comes from what's in the rig overhead.

Dancefloor lighting is what takes a reception from 'nice event' to 'where did the time go.' When the lights are moving, the colors are shifting, and everything is synced to the music — people stay on the floor.

We rig moving heads on truss or venue points above the dance floor. During dinner, they're warm and ambient. When the dancing starts, they come alive — sweeping beams, color shifts, strobe effects dialed to taste. We control all of it in real time.

We work with your DJ's setup to sync effects to music cues. First dance, grand entrance, high-energy moments — the lighting responds to what's happening on the floor, not the other way around.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does dance floor lighting cost for an event in Miami?

Dance floor lighting in Miami typically ranges from $500 to $2,000+ depending on the number of moving heads, fixture type, and event duration. Baseline Studio provides custom quotes based on your venue size and the look you're going for.

What is a moving head light and why does it matter?

Moving head fixtures are motorized lights that pan, tilt, and change color in response to the music. They create dynamic beams, chase patterns, and wash effects that standard par cans can't replicate. For dance floors, moving heads are what separate a professional setup from a rental package.

Do the lights sync to the music?

Yes. Our lighting technician or DJ programs the fixtures to respond to the beat and energy of the music in real time using a lighting controller. The light show builds with the music and hits on key moments — drops, build-ups, and peak energy points.

What's included in a dance floor lighting package?

A typical package includes 4–8 moving head fixtures, trussing or floor stands, a DMX controller, power distribution, full setup, and on-site operation. We also add wash lights, strobes, or laser effects depending on the package tier and your venue's permissions.

Can dance floor lighting be added to an existing DJ package?

Absolutely — most clients add dance floor lighting to their DJ or sound package. Baseline Studio handles all the production as a single team, so there's no coordination between separate vendors. Everything is programmed and operated together for a cohesive result.
