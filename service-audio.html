Audio is the foundation of every event we do. It doesn't matter how good the DJ is if the sound system is fighting the room. We engineer for the space — whether that's a 50-person garden wedding or a 1,000-person ballroom.

We run professional line array systems for larger venues and point-source systems for smaller, more intimate spaces. Every system is tuned on-site. We walk the room. We listen from the back. We adjust until it's right.

Outdoor events are where cheap audio systems break down. South Florida heat, humidity, and wind all impact sound. Our outdoor-rated systems handle the environment. We also bring weather-protected gear and plan for everything.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does PA system rental cost in Miami?

PA system rental in Miami typically ranges from $500 to $2,500+ depending on venue size, number of speakers, microphone needs, and whether you need a dedicated audio technician. Baseline Studio provides custom quotes — contact us with your event details for accurate pricing.

Do you provide a sound technician with the PA system?

Yes. Every Baseline Studio audio package includes a professional sound technician who handles setup, soundcheck, and live monitoring throughout your event. We don't just drop equipment off — we make sure every word and every note is heard exactly as intended.

What size PA system do I need for my event?

System size depends on your venue capacity and type. A cocktail hour for 100 guests needs a different setup than a 500-person ballroom or outdoor reception. We assess your specific venue and recommend the right speaker configuration — never undersized, never overkill.

Can the sound system handle both speeches and music?

Absolutely. Our systems are configured for full-range use — crystal-clear speech intelligibility for toasts and presentations, plus the full frequency response needed for music playback. We manage gain structure and EQ to ensure smooth transitions between both uses.

What microphone options do you offer?

We offer handheld wireless microphones, lavalier (clip-on) mics for speakers and officiants, and headset mics for performers. Microphone packages are included or can be added to any audio rental. We always have backup units on-site in case of any technical issue.
