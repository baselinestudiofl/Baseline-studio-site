Lighting

Stage Lighting Miami —
everyone on stage looks the part.

Spot lighting, wash fixtures, and follow spots for stages, ceremony arches, and presentation areas. Stage lighting tells the room where to look. Done right, your speakers look polished, your award recipients look significant, and the whole moment lands the way it's supposed to.

Get a Quote Get a Quote

When someone steps to a podium or walks down an aisle, the lighting tells the room how important that moment is. Flat overhead fluorescents say 'conference room.' A well-placed stage wash says 'this matters.'

We rig spot fixtures that eliminate the harsh shadows from above-head lighting — the kind that makes everyone look tired on video. Our stage lighting is designed to complement your venue's house lighting, not compete with it.

For ceremonies, we position fixtures to light the arch, the couple, and the officiant without washing out the photographer's shots. Lighting that works for the room AND the camera is a skill. We've had a lot of practice at it.

  • Color gels or full-spectrum LED
  • Ceremony and reception-specific setups

  • Photography-friendly lighting design

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does stage lighting cost for an event in Miami?

Stage and presentation lighting in Miami typically ranges from $600 to $2,500+ depending on stage size, number of fixtures, and whether front-wash, back-light, and accent lighting are all needed. Baseline Studio provides itemized quotes — contact us with your stage dimensions and event type.

Why does stage lighting matter for corporate events?

Proper stage lighting ensures speakers and presenters look polished and professional on camera and to the live audience. Without it, harsh shadows, uneven skin tones, and flat visibility undermine the credibility of your presenters. Good lighting makes every speaker look confident and authoritative.

What's included in a stage lighting setup?

A standard package includes front-wash fixtures for even facial illumination, back-lighting to separate speakers from the background, colored accent lights, a dimmer board for live control, and full rigging on truss or floor stands. We size the rig to your stage and audience distance.

Do you provide a lighting technician for the event?

Yes. A dedicated lighting technician manages the board throughout your event — adjusting intensity for different segments, hitting cues for video rolls or speaker introductions, and ensuring consistent quality from soundcheck through the final session. You don't have to worry about the board.

Can stage lighting work with an existing AV setup?

Yes. We integrate with in-house AV systems, LED walls, and projection setups. If you already have venue-provided AV, we can layer our stage lighting on top. We also handle full AV production if you need a single vendor for sound, lighting, and visual display.
