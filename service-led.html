An LED video wall does something a projection screen can't: it works in any lighting condition. Bright room, dark room, daylight — the picture stays crisp and vivid. For branded events and corporate productions, that matters.

We configure panels to fit your space. Standard wall, wide panoramic, stacked columns, freestanding towers — whatever the setup calls for. Content playback is handled by our team, so your graphics, logos, and videos look exactly as intended.

For weddings, LED walls are increasingly popular as scenic backdrops — behind the head table, at the ceremony altar, or as a photo backdrop. Custom graphics or looping content, color-matched to your palette.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does LED video wall rental cost in Miami?

LED video wall rental in Miami typically starts at $1,500 and scales based on wall size, resolution, and event duration. Our P3.9 panels deliver broadcast-quality clarity. Contact Baseline Studio for a custom quote based on your exact size and event requirements.

What content can be displayed on an LED video wall?

Our LED walls support live video feeds, brand graphics, sponsor logos, monograms, presentations, photo slideshows, ambient motion backgrounds, and social media displays. We can connect to laptops, cameras, or streaming sources — whatever your production requires.

How large can the LED video wall be?

Our P3.9 modular panels stack and connect cleanly, so we can build walls from 8 feet wide up to 20+ feet wide depending on your venue and budget. We'll recommend the right size based on viewing distance and room dimensions at your Miami venue.

Do I need to provide the content for the LED wall?

You can supply your own content or we can help create simple graphics and motion backgrounds. We handle all the technical setup — signal routing, processor configuration, and content playback — so you just need to tell us what you want displayed.

What types of events use LED video walls in Miami?

LED video walls are popular at corporate conferences, award galas, product launches, trade shows, weddings with live camera feeds, brand activations, and concert-style events. If you want your brand or content visible to a large room, an LED wall is the most impactful option.
