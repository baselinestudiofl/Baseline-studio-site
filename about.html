Who We Are

We're the crew
behind the night.

South Florida-based. Fully insured. Started behind a DJ booth at a family friend's wedding and never stopped. What began as a solo operation is now a full production crew covering everything from FOH sound to stage lighting to cold spark pyro.

Our Story

Started behind
the decks. Grew
into a full crew.

It started with a family friend asking me to DJ their wedding. I had never done it professionally before, so I learned, I showed up, I figured it out. The night went well. More importantly, I loved it. That was the beginning of Baseline Studio.

From there it grew, slowly at first, then all at once. Clients started asking for more: "Can you handle the sound?" "What about the lighting?" "We saw those cold sparks at someone else's wedding, can you do that?" We kept saying yes, kept investing in the equipment and the crew to back it up.

Now we're a full production operation. DJ, FOH sound, LED walls, uplighting, stage lighting, special effects, live musicians. We coordinate all of it and run it together at every event we take on. We've worked across Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach at weddings, quinceañeras, galas, corporate events, bar and bas mitzvahs, award shows, and private parties.

Every event has hiccups. That's just the reality. The job is making sure the client never knows about them. That's what we focus on.

The Founder

Saul Leon

Founder & Lead Producer, Baseline Studio

I started in this industry the way most people do — someone needed a DJ and I said yes before I fully knew what I was doing. That was my first wedding. I was hooked from the moment the room came alive.

Since then I've built Baseline Studio into a full production crew covering South Florida. I've personally produced hundreds of events across Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach — weddings, bar and bas mitzvahs, quinceañeras, corporate galas, award shows, and private parties at venues like the Ritz-Carlton Bal Harbour, The Bath Club, Turnberry Isle, and the Westin Diplomat.

What I learned early: the DJ is just one part of what makes a night work. Sound engineering, lighting design, special effects timing, crew coordination — all of it matters, and all of it has to work together. That's why I built a company that handles the whole production instead of just one piece of it.

I'm involved in every event we take on. Not just on paper — I'm on-site, running the show alongside the crew. That's not changing.
